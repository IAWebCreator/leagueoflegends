About CryptoLegends

CryptoLegends is a revolutionary meme token that brings together the excitement of gaming and the potential of cryptocurrency. Our token features legendary characters from gaming history, reimagined with a crypto twist. Join us on this epic quest to conquer the crypto realm!

TOKENOMICS

  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000,000 LEGEND
  • 5% Buy/Sell Tax: 2% to Liquidity, 3% to Marketing
  • 50% of Total Supply Burned at Launch
  • Anti-Whale Mechanism: Max Wallet 2% of Total Supply

HOW TO BUY

  1. Get a Wallet - Download a reputable Ethereum wallet like MetaMask or Trust Wallet.
  2. Acquire ETH - Purchase Ethereum (ETH) from a cryptocurrency exchange like Coinbase or Binance.
  3. Connect to a DEX - Connect your wallet to a decentralized exchange (DEX) like Uniswap or SushiSwap.
  4. Swap ETH for LEGEND - Use the DEX's swap feature to exchange your ETH for LEGEND tokens.
